### Design decisions recorded
1. Free-running 8-bit counter (no period reset) — simplest PWM implementation.
2. PWM compare is purely combinational for minimum latency.
3. Single DUTY register via wr_en (not APB) for simplicity.

### Risk notes
- DUTY=0 gives pwm_out permanently 0; DUTY=256 not representable in 8 bits.
- No synchronization on duty_in — assume stable when wr_en asserted.
